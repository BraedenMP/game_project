Stage 1

To win you must simply match 2 tiles! Each tile is hiding a letter, to reveal the letter you must click the tile. You may click 2 tiles (1 guess). If the tiles match you win $1. If your tiles don't match you won't win the prize and you must restart, losing all current winnings.
